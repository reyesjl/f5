Tours include visits to the following landmarks with exclusive access.

  • The Connemara region, including a walking tour through Medieval Galway and a visit to Kylemore Abbey.

  • Aviva Stadium Tour.

Rugby experience museum

The Rugby Experience Museum is a unique and immersive attraction that brings the rich history and vibrant culture of rugby to life. Nestled within the heart of Ireland, this museum offers an unparalleled journey through the sport’s evolution, from its early beginnings to its current global status.

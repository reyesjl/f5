About Us

Our mission is to promote and grow the game of rugby by providing accessible resources, organizing community events, and fostering a supportive environment for players of all levels.

Our vision is to become the leading platform for rugby enthusiasts, offering comprehensive services with continuous innovation to meet the evolving needs of the rugby community.

Key Initiatives

  • Events / Camps: General events, rugby camps, and featured events.
  • Blog and News: Coverage of explorative topics, unnoticed news, and interesting rugby developments.
  • Health Plans and Sessions: Free resources for all levels of the game, public plans, and tailored sessions with experts.
  • Team Tour Service: All-inclusive rugby tours with activities, exclusive tours, history lessons, and accommodations.
  • Custom Kit Service: Custom branded rugby kits with free design, simple process, and prompt delivery.

Conclusion

We aim to provide the highest quality in all our services, from accurate news to high-quality kits. Our goal is to grow positively and be the best in everything we do.

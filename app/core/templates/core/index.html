Maryland Camp

In Maryland, we hosted 2 rugby camps with 100+ players and world-class coaches. We collected testing data to track player progress and established an infrastructure for ongoing development. Players are paired with trainers on our platform and provided with free resources for continuous improvement.

Start with the players

Our jobs start at the grassroot levels because we know investing early allows players the greatest opportunity to succeed. Research shows that starting sports early enhances skill development, physical fitness, and cognitive and social growth. By focusing on young athletes, we help them build a strong foundation for future success.
